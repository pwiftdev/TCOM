Section 03
Color Usage Rules
✓ Do

Gold on Black always

Use Trench Gold (#C9A96E) exclusively on the black family of backgrounds. This is the logo's native contrast ratio and must be maintained.

✗ Don't

Gold on light backgrounds

Never place Trench Gold on white or light backgrounds. It loses contrast and breaks the brand identity. TCOM is a dark-first brand.

✓ Do

Warm tones only

Every color in the palette has a warm undertone (slight brown/yellow bias). Keep this in all custom additions. The brand lives in warm blacks and aged gold.

✗ Don't

Cool grays or pure white

Never use #FFFFFF, #F0F0F0, or any cool-tone gray. Pure white feels clinical and kills the trenches aesthetic. Always use Warm White (#F5F0E8).

✓ Do

Gold for primary actions only

Reserve Trench Gold for the single most important action on any screen (Join, Post, Create). This keeps its power. Use Ghost or Secondary buttons elsewhere.

✗ Don't

Overuse gold

If everything is gold, nothing is. Don't use gold for more than 2 UI elements in the same viewport. Scarcity is what makes it feel premium.

✓ Do

Dark mode is the only mode

TCOM is dark-only. Do not build a light mode. The dark aesthetic is core to the crypto trenches identity and the logo was designed for it.

✗ Don't

Bright accent colors

Avoid neon green, electric blue, or hot pink accents. If a status color is needed (error/success), keep it muted and earthy. The brand palette doesn't pop, it glows.
